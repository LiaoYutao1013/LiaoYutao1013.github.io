### Summary
Participated in the academic seminar focused on **asymmetric catalysis** and **green chemistry** in organic synthesis. The discussion primarily revolved around the development of new synthetic methodologies and their efficiency in creating complex bioactive molecules.

### Key Takeaways
* Engaged in discussion with Prof. Huang regarding the selective reduction of carboxylic acid derivatives.
* Explored the integration of automated synthesis in modern organic laboratories.
* Evaluated the economic and environmental impact of bio-refinery processes in modern organic synthesis.
