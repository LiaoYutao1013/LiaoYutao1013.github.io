### Core Performance Metrics
[cite_start]The seminar showcased the state-of-the-art research achievements in robotic positioning and manipulation, featuring industry-leading precision indicators:
* [cite_start]**Ultra-precision Parallel Platform**: Achieved a world-class **Resolution of 2 nm** with a **Repeatability of ±50 nm**, specifically designed for sub-micron operation environments.
* [cite_start]**Hybrid Mechanism Platform**: Demonstrated a **Repeatability of ±0.2 μm**, balancing workspace flexibility with high-precision output.
* [cite_start]**Micro-gripping Technology**: Developed fully compliant mechanisms with an adjustable **opening range of 30-50 μm**, enabling non-destructive handling of mesoscopic parts.

### Strategic Application Fields
[cite_start]The discussed technologies have been successfully deployed in several "National Major Projects," proving their reliability in extreme scenarios:

1. **Laser Nuclear Fusion (ICF)**:
   * [cite_start]**Task**: High-precision assembly of fuel target spheres and micro-tubes.
   * [cite_start]**Impact**: Critical for Inertial Confinement Fusion research, involving complex spatial alignment at the hundred-micron scale.

2. **Photonic Chips & Optoelectronics**:
   * [cite_start]**Task**: Automated alignment of fiber arrays for next-generation photonic integrated circuits.
   * [cite_start]**Impact**: Collaborated with industry leaders like **Huawei** to solve the "bottleneck" problem of high-efficiency optical coupling.

### Future Outlook: AI-Driven Embodied Intelligence
[cite_start]Beyond hardware, the talk explored the frontier of **Embodied Intelligence** and **VLA (Vision-Language-Action) Models**:
* [cite_start]**Task Decomposition**: Using Large Language Models (LLMs) to translate natural language instructions into executable assembly trajectories.
* [cite_start]**Self-Adaptive Control**: Implementing reinforcement learning and dense point cloud vision to enhance the robot's perception of 3D mesoscopic environments.
* **Industrial Synergy**: Bridging the gap between general AI and specialized manufacturing scenarios to build a robust "Micro-assembly Knowledge Base".
